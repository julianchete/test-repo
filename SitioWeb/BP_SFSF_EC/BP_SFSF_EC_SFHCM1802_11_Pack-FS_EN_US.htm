Offering a new approach to core HR, the SuccessFactors Employee Central solution is the industry's only core HR solution that provides comprehensive, integrated core HR capabilities specifically designed for business execution.

This enterprise-grade core HR solution is driven by effective dated entries and provides support for any combination of business units, geos, or cost centers to model and manage any workforce. It enables HR to quickly implement any job structure without codes, spreadsheets, or offline processes, and supports any pay structure, enabling companies to vary pay by geo, job class, grade, or legal entities. The solution facilitates reporting and industry compliance by ensuring all user actions are saved and easily accessible through point-in-time reporting and easy audit trail reports.

With the use of preconfigured content, you can accelerate the implementation and get optimal time to value of your core HR processes in the cloud.
